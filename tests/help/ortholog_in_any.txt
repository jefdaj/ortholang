ortholog_in_any : og faa.list -> str.list.list

where
  og = any orthogroup (ofr, spr, or gcr)
  faa = FASTA amino acid
  str = string

Arguments:
  orthogroup finder results
  list of proteomes (fasta files)

Returns: list of lists of strings

Filter a list of orthogroups to keep the ones with an ortholog in one of the given proteomes.
